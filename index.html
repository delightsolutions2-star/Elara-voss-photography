"A photograph is a secret about a secret. The more it tells you, the less you know." — Diane Arbus

Based in Santa Fe, New Mexico, I've spent over a decade documenting the quiet intimacy of human experience — from sun-bleached desert elopements to the tender chaos of growing families.

My work lives in the warmth of natural light, the honesty of an unguarded moment, and the feeling that time slowed just long enough to hold something true.
